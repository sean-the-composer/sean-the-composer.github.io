Questions?

We’ve got answers

  • Am I eligible for this plan?

    If you are a student enrolled at an accredited college or university, and above the age of 18, then yes. You can get Premium Student for up to 4 years.

  • How do you verify that I’m a student?

    We verify your enrollment status through a third-party service called SheerID. Learn more.

  • What happens to my account when I graduate or leave school?

    You’ll continue to get access to Premium Student, for up to 12 months from the date you subscribed or last re-verified, while it’s available. If you’re no longer a student at the end of that period, you’ll no longer be eligible for Premium Student. Your subscription will then automatically switch to a Premium Individual plan at A$13.99/month.

  • What will I be charged and when?

    You will be charged A$6.99/month. If the price changes in the future, we’ll notify you first. You can cancel anytime on your Spotify Account page.
